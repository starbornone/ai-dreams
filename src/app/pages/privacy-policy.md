**AI Dreams** respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle any personal information collected through your use of this site.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Analytics Data

We use Vercel Analytics to collect anonymous usage data about how visitors interact with our website. This helps us understand site performance and improve user experience. The analytics service collects:

- **Page views**: Which pages are visited
- **Visit duration**: How long visitors stay on the site
- **Referral sources**: How visitors found our site
- **Device information**: Browser type, device type, and screen size
- **Geographic region**: Country-level location data (not precise locations)

Vercel Analytics is privacy-focused and does not use cookies for tracking. All data collected is anonymised and aggregated. For more details about Vercel's data practices, please visit their [Analytics documentation](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics) .

## 2. External Links

Our site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies if you choose to visit them.

## 3. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed to reflect any changes in our practices. Any updates will be posted on this page, with the last updated date at the top.
