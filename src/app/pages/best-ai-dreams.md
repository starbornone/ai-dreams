## title: Best of AI Dreams

While the posts below provide a starting point for exploring various concepts, for a more in-depth study, consider consulting my curated list of recommended non-fiction books found in the [Book Recommendations: The Non-Fiction Edition](https://www.aidreams.world/pages/book-recommendations-non-fiction-edition); these books offer a more comprehensive understanding of the various subject matter.

### Economics

- [Cyberpunk Is an Optimistic Depiction of a Capitalist Future](https://www.aidreams.world/posts/cyberpunk-optimistic-depiction-capitalist-future) discusses some of the consequences of using an outdated perception of a dystopian future.

- [The Foundations of Value, Price, and Profit](https://www.aidreams.world/posts/foundations-value-price-profit) examines the fundamental aspects of capitalism and where value comes from.

More posts can be found in the [economics category](https://www.aidreams.world/categories/economics).

### Life

- [My 10 Rules for a More Fulfilling Life](https://www.aidreams.world/posts/10-rules-fulfilling-life) gives insight into how I face the world via a set of rules that help me be the best version of myself I can be.

- [Reflecting on My Life: The 2022 Edition](https://www.aidreams.world/posts/reflecting-life-2022-edition) goes into some major revelations in my life, from overcoming anxiety to getting better at saving money.

More posts can be found in the [life category](https://www.aidreams.world/categories/life).

### Games

- [Defining Games: Exploring the Boundaries of Play and Imagination](https://www.aidreams.world/posts/defining-games-exploring-boundaries-play-imagination) ponders the concept of a game and how we’ve now grouped so many interactive experiences under the one banner.

- [Dark Patterns: An Introduction to the Darker Side of Engagement](https://www.aidreams.world/posts/dark-patterns-introduction-darker-side-engagement) explains what a dark pattern is, what types there are, and why they’re manipulative.

- [Gamification and Exploitative Tactics in Games and Apps](https://www.aidreams.world/posts/gamification-exploitative-tactics-games-apps) expanding on the post about dark patterns, here we look at more specific ways in which they manifest.

- [Stop Trying To Force Plays: The Application of Strategy and Tactics in Games](https://www.aidreams.world/posts/stop-trying-force-plays-application-strategy-tactics-games) goes all Art of War on gaming to discuss the role of strategy and tactics in improving gaming skills.

More posts can be found in the [games category](https://www.aidreams.world/categories/games).

### Philosophy

- [Embracing Curiosity: The Power of Never-Ending Questions](https://www.aidreams.world/posts/embracing-curiosity-power-never-ending-questions) examines one of the most vital aspects of maintaining a healthy intellect: the question.

More posts can be found in the [philosophy category](https://www.aidreams.world/categories/philosophy).

### Politics

- [Escaping Capitalist Realism: Why a Post-Capitalist World Feels Unimaginable](https://www.aidreams.world/posts/escaping-capitalist-realism-post-capitalist-world-feels-unimaginable), like it says on the tin, examines why it’s so difficult to imagine a world without capitalism.

- [From Spectacle to Substance: Reframing Political Discourse](https://www.aidreams.world/posts/spectacle-substance-reframing-political-discourse) aims to get one into the mindset of how to think about politics without falling for media sensationalism.

- [The Power of Observation: Surveillance and Sousveillance](https://www.aidreams.world/posts/power-observation-surveillance-sousveillance) analyse the difference between surveillance and sousveillance, and the impact both have within the framework of our society.

More posts can be found in the [politics category](https://www.aidreams.world/categories/politics).

### Technology

- [Ethics in Programming: When Your IDE Has Ideology](https://www.aidreams.world/posts/ethics-programming-ide-ideology) talks about the issues we don’t think about when we’re working on a project as software engineers and how it can shape our society for better or worse.

- [The New Normal: Our Digital Dependency](https://www.aidreams.world/posts/new-normal-digital-dependency) examines the amalgamation of power into the hands of a handful of tech giants and the potential consequences of our reliance on them.

More posts can be found in the [technology category](https://www.aidreams.world/categories/technology).
